LasagnaB

Description

LasagnaB is a variation of the classic lasagna, featuring different ingredients and flavors.

This version includes a mix of vegetables and a creamy white sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, spinach)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Step

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with salt, pepper, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, vegetable mixture, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with Alfredo sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
